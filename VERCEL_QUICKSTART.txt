🔐 ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES (.env'den kopyala):

   ✅ DB_HOST
   ✅ DB_USER
   ✅ DB_PASSWORD
   ✅ DB_NAME
   ✅ DB_PORT
   ✅ MONGODB_URI
   ✅ JWT_SECRET
   ✅ SESSION_SECRET
   ✅ GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID
   ✅ GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET
   ✅ GOOGLE_CALLBACK_URL (domain deploy sonrası güncelle)
   ✅ AI_PROVIDER=groq
   ✅ GROQ_API_KEY

   ⚠️  Her variable için Production, Preview, Development SEÇ!

✅ DEPLOY SONRASI:

   1. Domain'i kopyala (örn: tercih-ai.vercel.app)
   
   2. Google OAuth Callback güncelle:
      Google Cloud Console → OAuth 2.0 → Redirect URIs
      → https://[domain].vercel.app/auth/google/callback
   
   3. Vercel Environment Variable güncelle:
      GOOGLE_CALLBACK_URL → yeni domain
   
   4. Redeploy
